Competitive Pricing Strategy Strategy

The best quality about MelziTech.

At MelziTech, we take pride in offering top-notch quality paired with a competitive pricing strategy. Our global presence allows us to assemble a team of skilled software developers from various countries. By strategically assigning your project to a team in a region with a currency weaker than yours, we significantly reduce your overall costs.

How It Works:

Let’s consider an example. Suppose you’re based in Saudi Arabia, and the estimated cost of our services is $10,000 in your local currency. Opting for our services means we can assign a team of developers from the US, where the currency is approximately three times weaker. As a result, you pay not the original $10,000 but a reduced amount of $3,000.
